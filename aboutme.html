Games that inspire me!

MLBB

Mobile Legends is a fast-paced 5v5 multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game designed for mobile devices. With a wide roster of heroes and intense team-based gameplay, it challenges players to work together strategically to defeat their opponents. I enjoy it for its competitive nature and how it sharpens my sense of game balance, character design, and real-time decision-making.

HKIA

Hello Kitty Island Adventure is a cozy life-simulation game that blends adorable aesthetics with relaxing gameplay. Players explore a vibrant island, build friendships with iconic Sanrio characters, and customize their own spaces. I appreciate how this game emphasizes player comfort, charm, and emotional connection—important elements I aim to bring into my own creative work.

REPO

REPO is a fast-paced 3D action-platformer developed by students from DigiPen Institute of Technology. Set in a vibrant, stylized world, the game puts players in the role of a futuristic repo agent tasked with retrieving valuable items through dynamic movement, combat, and exploration. What stands out in REPO is its fluid traversal system and polished level design, which make the gameplay feel energetic and rewarding. As an aspiring game developer, I find REPO inspiring for how it balances action, mobility, and world-building — all while being a student-led project.
