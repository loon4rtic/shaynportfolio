About Me

Get to know me

I’m Shayn, a game developer and designer based in Singapore, currently interning at Hiverlab as a Unity developer. I recently completed my Diploma in Game Development & Technology at Nanyang Polytechnic, where I built everything from a multiplayer co-op game to an educational VR experience for children with special needs. I’m drawn to games that make you feel something — the dread of a horror game, the quiet comfort of a cozy indie. That tension between emotion and interaction is what drives how I think about design: every mechanic, every scene should serve the experience. Outside of game projects, I also build for the web, which is how this portfolio came to life. I’m always looking for new problems to solve and new worlds to build — take a look at my projects to see what I’ve been working on.
